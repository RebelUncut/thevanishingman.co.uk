In a city consumed by civil war, a black ops soldier risks everything by kidnapping a prisoner about to be publicly executed.

As special forces close in he interrogates his hostage in an attempt to uncover the truth about 'The Vanishing Man' - a man held responsible for starting the war.

The Vanishing Man is a short film based on an original feature length script. The challenge of the shorter narrative has allowed us to further examine and develop the main characters and core themes, whilst showcasing much of the style and world that the feature film will encompass.

A work-in-progress cut of the film has already been accepted for the Festival De Cannes 2015 Short Film Corner, and we have high hopes for the finished film on the festival circuit, whilst we complete the development of the feature script.

  • Robbie Brown is an experienced freelance writer-director with a background in editing, working on projects for broadcast, online content, commercials & corporate promos. His film credits include three short films: The Vanishing Man being his fourth with a feature script in development.

    • Mr. Orange Short 2’30” - 2011 Director/Writer
    • Satsuma Short 9’ - 2002 Director
    • Close Call Short 2’ - 2001 Director/Writer
    "I love films with a positive message, where regardless of the darkness of the setting, there is a sense of hope. At it's core, The Vanishing Man deals with oppression, love and evolution and how the three could be connected."

  • Julia Higginbottom is an experienced producer with two features and six shorts under her belt. Her background is diverse - ranging from commercial pieces to narrative film and event delivery. She has a can-do attitude and will always strive to make a project exceed expectations.
